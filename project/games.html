Premise of the Game Players claim their set of high (stripes) or low (solids) and shoot them in, taking turns when they miss a shot. Once their set is all in, the player must call the pocket for the 8-Ball and shoot it in. Players must shoot the balls in order from 1 to 9, whoever pockets the 9-Ball wins. Players must alternate shooting in red balls with numbered balls for points. Player with the most points wins. Players take turns shooting as many balls in as possible, gaining one point per ball, up until an agreed upon number of points. Players choose their set of balls (high, mid, or low) and shoot the other players balls in. The last player with balls on the table wins.
